 Social Media Responsibility Checklist

 ✅ Do’s

1. Think before posting anything (once it’s online, it stays there)
2. Keep your accounts private and protect your personal information
3. Check if information is true before sharing it
4. Be respectful while commenting or chatting with others
5. Use social media in a positive way like learning or networking



 ❌ Don’ts

1. Don’t share personal details like passwords, phone number, or address
2. Don’t post anything offensive or inappropriate
3. Avoid bullying or making fun of others online
4. Don’t click on random or suspicious links
5. Don’t waste too much time scrolling and ignoring your studies
