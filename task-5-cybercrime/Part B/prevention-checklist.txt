Stay Safe Online Checklist

✅ Tips for College Students

Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts. Avoid easy ones like 123456 or your name.
Turn on two-factor authentication for email, social media, and banking apps.
Don’t share OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone, even if they seem official.
Always check links before clicking, especially from unknown messages or emails.
Keep your social media accounts private and be careful about what personal info you post.
Double-check the receiver’s name and UPI ID before sending money.
Only approve UPI collect requests from people you completely trust.
Avoid using public WiFi for banking or online payments.
