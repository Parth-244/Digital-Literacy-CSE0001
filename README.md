## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on improving digital literacy skills and understanding how to use digital tools safely and effectively. It covers important areas like online safety, professional presence, basic problem solving, and awareness about cyber threats. The tasks are divided into different modules, each helping in building practical knowledge step by step.

---

## 📂 Modules / Tasks

### 🔹 Task 1: Infographic on Digital Literacy

In this task, I created an infographic slide covering the basics of digital literacy. It included topics like what digital literacy is, essential digital tools, safe internet practices, building a professional online presence, and basic email etiquette. This helped in understanding the foundation of using technology properly.

---

### 🔹 Task 2: Student Digital Profile

In this task, I built my digital profile by creating accounts on LinkedIn, GitHub, and Kaggle. This was useful for starting my professional online presence and exploring platforms used for networking, coding, and data-related work.

---

### 🔹 Task 3: Skill Building and Awareness Quiz

**Part A:**
I created an account on HackerRank and solved beginner-level coding challenges to improve my problem-solving skills.

**Part B:**
I made a Google Form on digital literacy awareness which included questions related to online safety and basic digital knowledge.

---

### 🔹 Task 4: Email Etiquette and Social Media Responsibility

**Part A:**
I wrote two formal emails:

* One to a professor requesting an extension for an assignment
* One to an internship coordinator expressing interest in a summer internship

This helped me understand proper email structure and professional communication.

**Part B:**
I created a checklist with 5 Do’s and 5 Don’ts for responsible social media use as a college student.

---

### 🔹 Task 5: Cyber Awareness

**Part A:**
I wrote a case study on cyberbullying, explaining what it is, how it happens, who it targets, and its effects.

**Part B:**
I created a “Stay Safe Online” checklist with 8 practical tips, including safe internet usage and UPI/payment safety.

---

## ✅ Conclusion

Through this project, I learned the importance of digital literacy, online safety, and building a professional digital identity. It also helped me understand real-world cyber issues and how to stay safe online.
